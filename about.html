PDX.FUTBOL

How to use this site

Looking for a pickup game or just trying to find your field? PDX.FUTBOL is helps you find your field, directions, and the time it will take to drive there. Search for a field or address and click the ⚽️ to get directions and drive time. Don't see your field or want to make a suggestion? Email us or suggest an edit for the specific field.

Who

I got tired of looking up fields so I thought I'd make this myself. I work for Mapbox and I'm a futbol nut and map nerd.

Dev

This site is open source and written in vanilla JavaScript and HTML. Please see the GitHub repo to contribute or open issues. The map is made with Mapbox and uses the Directions API. All fields are estimated from OpenStreetMap, an open source map data source.
